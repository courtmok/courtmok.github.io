I’m a data scientist with a background in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I graduated from Barnard College of Columbia University in the spring of 2020, majoring in Computer Science with a focus on Intelligent Systems. I developed my love for data during an "Introduction to Data Visualizations" course my senior year of college, where I discovered the innovative stories and real world impact one can create with data. Post graduation I took an intensive Data Science course at General Assembly in order to learn more about working with data and develop my applicable skills.

I grew up in very international environments

and a passion for developing innovative data stories. I have a passion for coding and to continue learning new libraries and develop my coding skills. I am interested in the intersection of data decision making and strive to develop creative and efficient solutions to challenging problems. I have experience self managing large solo projects, but I also thrive in cooperative environments as a leader and as a team player.

I love learning languages whether it's spoken languages or a computer languages. I grew up speaking Mandarin and Cantonese and in college I picked up Japanese and Korean.
